  • The Team

    We are Carlton and Julia Riffel. A husband and wife creative team based out of Wilmington NC. Working together we contrast and compliment each other to make beautiful work with intention and purpose. Whether hanging out with friends, drinking coffee on the porch of our little historic house, or playing with our fluffy orange cat Hans; we are grateful to enjoy God's beauty all around us. Two is better than one not just in life, but also in solving creative problems and making incredible photos, videos and designs.

  • Carlton

    I am passionate about creating. Although I have been interested in taking photos from a young age, earning my B.A. in Studio Art honed my ability to see and produce beautiful imagery. Doing so not only takes a "good eye" but also a skill for creative problem solving - which I love. Throughout the school year you will find me teaching art at Wilmington Christian Academy. I am always excited to see my students grow as they learn about art, beauty, and communication. When not at work, I enjoy letter-pressing paper, playing my guitar, inventing my alarm clock, making art, or listening to audio books. I am so thankful that God allowed me to marry such a wonderful teammate and friend.

  • Julia

    You will most likely find me making something or drawing up plans to make something. My hands are always busy trying to keep up with my brain. Whether it's jewelry for Spectrum, projects for my home, food for my table or pictures for my blog, I find joy in making something new.

  • Experience

    Having photographed over 30 weddings, numerous videos, and design jobs large and small; we have the professional expertise to create authentic original work. Our experience with multiple disciplines gives us a unique perspective on project possibilities, clear communication, and purpose driven decisions resulting in client satisfaction. We love to work with passionate clientele that share our love for visionary work.
